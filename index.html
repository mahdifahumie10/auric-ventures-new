About Us

Spaces made to be lived in, not just looked at.

As a CIDB-licensed contractor (0120250625-WP157028), Auric Ventures provides expert consultation and practical solutions for every stage of your project. Our services include house renovation, structural works, home extensions, planning consultation, architectural drawings, and assistance with planning approvals and authority submissions.

From the initial concept to project completion, we are committed to delivering quality,professionalism, and attention to detail.

Our Vision

At Auric Ventures, customer satisfaction is our priority. We are committed to quality workmanship, transparent communication, and completing every project safely, on time, and within budget

Our Mission

To be a long-term and trusted partner for our clients with 24-hour service. We proudly serve clients throughout Kuala Lumpur and Selangor, with selected projects in Johor Bahru, Ipoh, and Penang

Our Experience

With over 10 years of industry experience, our skilled team delivers high-quality renovation, structural, and construction solutions tailored to every client's needs

Our Process

From first enquiry to handover.

Every renovation follows a clear, structured process — so you always know what's happening, what's next, and when to expect it.

01

Customer Enquiry

You reach out with your renovation idea, no matter how early-stage.

02

Site Inspection

We visit in person to assess the space and understand the scope.

03

Consultation

We discuss your goals, budget, and preferences in detail.

04

Quotation

A clear, itemized quote with no hidden costs, sent for your review.

05

Design & Planning

Layouts, materials, and timelines are finalized before any work begins.

06

Approval (if required)

We handle any necessary permits or local authority approvals on your behalf.

07

Construction & Renovation

Our team executes the work with regular progress updates along the way.

08

Final Inspection

A thorough walkthrough to confirm everything meets our quality standard.

09

Project Handover

Keys back in your hands, with your space ready to live in.
