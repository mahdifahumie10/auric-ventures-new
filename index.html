Our Process

From first enquiry to handover.

Every renovation follows a clear, structured process — so you always know what's happening, what's next, and when to expect it.

01

Customer Enquiry

You reach out with your renovation idea, no matter how early-stage.

02

Site Inspection

We visit in person to assess the space and understand the scope.

03

Consultation

We discuss your goals, budget, and preferences in detail.

04

Quotation

A clear, itemized quote with no hidden costs, sent for your review.

05

Design & Planning

Layouts, materials, and timelines are finalized before any work begins.

06

Approval (if required)

We handle any necessary permits or local authority approvals on your behalf.

07

Construction & Renovation

Our team executes the work with regular progress updates along the way.

08

Final Inspection

A thorough walkthrough to confirm everything meets our quality standard.

09

Project Handover

Keys back in your hands, with your space ready to live in.
